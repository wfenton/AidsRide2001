Late in the fall of 2000, a group of us at Latitude decided it would be fun to take part in the 2001 AIDS rides. We would have to raise a ton of money, and train all spring/summer for a 7-day, 575 mile bike ride. 7 of us signed up for the Montana ride (myself, Doug Erpenbeck, Bart Narter, John Marcone, Jessica Fewless, Tiffany Walker, and Brett Prince). 2 of us signed up for the California ride (Wesley Yu and John Hollwedel). Eric Weisberg signed up to help crew for the Montana ride. 


We went on training ride after training ride, from February to July, all around the bay area and beyond. We begged and pleaded for donations from our co-workers, family members, friends, and strangers. 


Wes and John completed the California ride and came back with stories, and tips for the rest of us. At the end of July, having reached our goal of $3400 in donations for each of us, 8 of us embarked on our adventure.
